My Story

I found my inspiration and motivation for Web Development during my community college days!

Those days, I consistently tried to look for schools to transfer. I visited more than 10 school's websites every day to check out what they have to offer. Some of these school's websites really stuck in my mind. Although I have never visited the schools, their websites gave me such a good experience that I wanted to be a part of them.

Since then, I started to become more curious about Web Development. My goal naturally becomes being able to develop and deliver a website that is impressive, attractive, and impactful as such websites that have had the same impact on me.

I have been doing and learning to improve my Web Development skills from a variety of sources. I love looking at my website acting responsive and keeping its good shape on many different devices.

Besides Web Development, I'm also trained to specialize in Business Analytics and Information Systems with practical knowledge in projects using SQL, C#, Python, and many more tools as well as methodologies.

I can work under pressure, multi-task, and maintain high accuracy at the same time. I am an open-mind listener who is willing to learn and enjoy helping others out. I believe that "you never fail until you stop trying," and I always do my best to learn new things every day and keep myself being productive.

I am bilingual in English and Vietnamese. I care about Children, Environment, Economy, Poverty, Mental Health, and Education. I'm here not only to look for better opportunities but also to connect to people with a similar mindset who care about similar things as me.

Please feel free to reach out to me if you feel like I could help you in any way! Thank you very much!
